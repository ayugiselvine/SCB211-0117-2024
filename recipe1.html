Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts (sliced thinly)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 yellow bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken slices and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Toss in bell peppers and broccoli, stir-frying for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Mix in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir well to coat.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with rice or noodles!

Nutritional Information

Calories: 320 | Protein: 28g | Carbs: 12g | Fat: 18g
