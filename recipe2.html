Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon (diced)
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic (optional, whole)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (for pasta water)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente (about 8-10 minutes).
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add pancetta and garlic (if using) and cook until crispy, about 5 minutes. Remove garlic afterward.
  3. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  4. Drain spaghetti, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water, then add spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta.
  5. Remove skillet from heat and quickly pour in the egg mixture, tossing to create a creamy sauce. Add a splash of reserved pasta water if needed.
  6. Season generously with black pepper and serve immediately.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 550 | Protein: 22g | Carbs: 60g | Fat: 25g
